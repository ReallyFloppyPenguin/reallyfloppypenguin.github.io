When AI takes all of your jobs

This is a in-depth blog about what happens when AI takes all of the jobs

Back to Blog

The advancements in recent AI models (the likes of ChatGPT leaves us with questions:

  • Will I still have a job

  • If not, how do I make money

  • What will happen to The Economy

  • How can I prepare?

Question 1: "Will I still have a job"

To be honest, probably not, you will most likely lose your job by 2027-2029, possible 2026. Of course, it depends on what job you have, for example:

  • Builders will go out the window, with AI robots taking over

  • Lawyers will be safe due to the possible biases of an LLM

  • Tech jobs are already disappearing

Well, how can I keep my job?

Erm, you can't. AI is unstoppable.

Question 2: If I lose my job, how will I make money?

So, there are a few answers to this one:

  • You can hope that Governments give some sort of benefits system to all who have lost their job to AI, but, this should not be your only hope.

  • You can build your own B2B (Business-to-Business) Service, you could sell this to other companies looking to replace employees with AI.

  • You can start saving like hell.

Question 3: What will happen to The Economy

Nobody really knows for sure, but I will say, that Governments will have a crazy headache trying to sort all of this out.

They'll most likely have to put some sort of tariff on AI for an employee
